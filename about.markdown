I'm a senior machine learning engineer and AI product builder, currently building **DentClarity**, a privacy-first dental revenue intelligence platform for claim leakage audits.

Before DentClarity, I worked on ads ranking and retrieval at **Meta**, building large-scale ML systems for WhatsApp Status ads, Feed/Reels clicks, and Shops Ads. Earlier, I led ML systems for high-frequency trading at **Redwood Capital**, where the work spanned alpha modeling, reinforcement learning, real-time drift monitoring, and production trading infrastructure.

The common thread is turning ambiguous business problems into reliable ML products:

**DentClarity** - Google Cloud AI agent platform for secure dental revenue-leakage audits, managed de-identification, audit orchestration, LLM-assisted extraction, deterministic recovery analysis, and pilot-ready reports.

**Ads ranking and retrieval** - Multi-objective ranking systems, deep conversion models, retrieval precision, experiment strategy, and high-throughput serving.

**Quantitative ML** - Market microstructure, statistical arbitrage, reinforcement learning, time-series forecasting, and low-latency inference.

**AI agents** - Automated workflows for model iteration, launch protocols, browser automation, research synthesis, and operational decision support.
